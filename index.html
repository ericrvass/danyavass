ABOUT ME · PORTFOLIO
Danya Vass is a full-time, non-union voice talent who records voiceovers for commercials, eLearning, documentaries, IVR, corporate training, medical narrations, and more! Her smooth voice and sincere, conversational style are perfect for a wide array of voiceover projects. Danya works from her home broadcast quality studio out of Atlanta, GA, where she is able to to utilize different recording and delivery options such as on site recording, phone patch, and remote live direction. Danya provides prompt and professional voiceover services.

Danya’s voice over credits include projects for major brands, including Wise Potato Chips and Papa Johns, as well as hundreds of other commercials and narrations. She also has extensive experience behind the microphone during her tenure with various radio stations in the Southeast.

Danya’s diverse background enhances her voiceover flexibility, allowing her to narrate a broad array of projects as the believable girl-next-door, or convey a warm, comforting, youthful, and storyteller type read. She looks forward to working with you on your next project!
